Private Defense in Criminal Cases

Every person suspected of an alleged criminal act has the right - from the first moment they realize they are being investigated - to designate a defense of their confidence.

Our studio is characterized by offering personalized advice. Assuming an appropriate defensive strategy, in accordance with the theory of the case that the situation warrants.

Situations that allow one to presume that one is the subject of a criminal investigation are, for example, becoming aware that a complaint has been made against one, a search of one's home, a court summons, an inhibition of goods, a prohibition of approach, among others. In these cases, it is essential to resort as soon as possible to a defense that provides comprehensive advice.

This will allow us to outline a defensive strategy that protects the rights of the person, their property and their honor in an effective manner from the beginning of the case.

For this reason, our studio offers personalized advice. Assuming an appropriate defensive strategy, in accordance with the theory of the case that the situation warrants.
