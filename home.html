Practice Areas

We perform efficiently in each of the following areas. Providing our service permanently available.

Private defense in criminal cases

Every person suspected of an alleged criminal act has the right - from the first moment they realize they are being investigated - to designate a defense of their confidence.

Criminal litigation in adversarial system

The reform of the Procedural Codes (for provincial and federal crimes) led to the progressive implementation of the adversarial system in which orality prevails as a rule of criminal proceedings.

Cybercrime

The advancement of technological tools and their use in our daily lives forces those who operate the law to adapt to the new reality.
